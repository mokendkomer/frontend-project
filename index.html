ABOUT US

Great things are done by a series of small things brought together

Here at Sirona Hygiene Foundation, we are driven by a single goal; to do our part in making the world a better place for all women irrespective of race, caste, social or economic background. Our decision making process is informed by comprehensive empirical studies and high quality data evaluation. We strive to build productive relationships and make a positive impact with all of our pursuits.
